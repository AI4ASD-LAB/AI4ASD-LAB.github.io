Xiaoyue Ma is a master's candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Zhejiang Normal University. She received her Bachelor's degree from North China Electric Power University (Science and Technology College) in 2023 and started her master's program in 2024.

Her research focuses on early screening of autism spectrum disorder (ASD) in children, leveraging AI techniques to analyze behavioral patterns, social interactions, and multimodal data (e.g., eye gaze, facial expressions, vocalizations). She aims to develop non-invasive, scalable, and objective screening tools that can assist clinicians and caregivers in early identification of ASD, ultimately enabling timely intervention. Her work bridges computer vision, affective computing, and child development psychology.
