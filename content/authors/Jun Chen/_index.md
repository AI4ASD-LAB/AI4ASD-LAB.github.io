Jun Chen is a Ph.D. and a "Shuanglong Scholar" Distinguished Professor at Zhejiang Normal University, as well as a master's supervisor in the School of Computer Science. He received his Ph.D. in Control Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University (2020–2024). His research focuses on deep learning compression and acceleration, deep learning theory, distributed optimization, and manifold learning. He was awarded the First Prize of Zhejiang Science and Technology Progress Award in 2021. In the past five years, he has published over 30 papers in top-tier journals such as JMLR, TPAMI, TNNLS, TSP, TOG, and in leading conferences including ICCV, ECCV, AAAI, ICLR, and NeurIPS, with 18 as first/corresponding author.
